The following is a map in the style of 80s pop art colors and Art Deco architechture. The original concept draws from South Beach in Miami, FL.

The theme I recreated here is South Beach in Miami, Florida circa the 1980s. South Beach is a unique location to see countless examples of 80s Pop Art colors and sytling, synthesized with Art Deco architecture. This aesthetic draws on inspiration from photos of South Beach itself, as well as clich√© 80s Pop Art pictures, and a Miami Vice poster. Typography will be the anchor for the Art Deco section of the map, with custom fonts. Icons will be Aet Deco in style, but the color scheme will match the pop art style.

Part of the inspiration is a drawn poster for the Miami Vice TV show that illustrates the colors used by the cartographer.

The inspiration for how the roads look comes from this picture of a grid system laid out in front of a city.

The style of the icons and textures match the art deco architecture and the colors match the palate of the 80s pop art theme.
